Fastest Way To Gain Muscles


Gaining muscles and getting bigger like a Hulk or some famous Bodybuilders like Arnold, Ronnie Coleman is every kids dream, and to achieve it in their teenage is what they desire but nobody has an idea about how to achieve it or the right way, the fastest way to achieve it. So, here is how you can gain muscles the fastest way possible firstly you have to get this clear that getting bigger muscles without using steroids is impossible because it is not, anyone can achieve it. It may take some time but still, you can achieve the desired body you just have to follow the correct steps.

You have to know your fat percentage if you have more than 20 percent of fat then you have to cut off your calorie intake and you have to do cardio starting with jumping jacks 3 sets of 50 repetitions each with 30-second rest in between sets, Burpees 3 sets of 30 repetitions each, rope jumping 3 sets of 100 repetitions each with 30-second rest, Alternate knee raises 3 sets of 10 minutes each set, Squats 3 sets of 20 repetitions each with 30-second rest, Push-ups 3 sets of 10 repetitions each if you are not able to do 10 repetitions then make it to 5. By doing these Cardio will burn your calories as fast as possible. Now the best thing to do is to train your muscles by joining a nearby gym and starting weight training. I have seen many people in the gym wasting their time and money by taking so much rest that their muscles come to a resting point after which they cannot do any more repetitions, which is an obstacle for you in this journey of gaining muscles as fast as possible. You have to take as little rest as you can and drinking small gulps of water during your rest, which will make you hydrated and hence enhancing your performance.

Best way to train your muscles is by following a simple routine that is for a beginner you have to do compound exercises that is training your overall body. Nowadays every gym has these compound machines even if it doesn’t then follow these workouts, firstly start with lat pull down 3 sets of 8-10 repetitions each and always take rest in between these workouts as little as possible. Then do chest press 3 sets of 8-10 repetitions each, shoulder press 3 sets of10 repetitions each, alternate Bicep’s curls and Triceps parallel bar dips, do both of them with 3 sets of 10-12 repetitions and weighted squats 3 sets of 10 repetitions each.

as you gain some strength and you are at your intermediate level, you have to follow this workout routine, on Monday train your Back muscle groups and biceps, on Tuesday go for Chest and Triceps, on Wednesday train your legs and shoulders, and then repeat the same for Thursday, Friday, Saturday. On Sundays you take rest as much as you can, the more your muscle gets its rest the bigger it will grow. Now to the diet portion, you need to eat low carbs and high protein diets include one non-veg item in your meal eat as frequently as you can in a day take small meals. You can take protein supplements to fulfill your daily protein intake but that’s not compulsory you can fulfill it with natural sources too. Always take 8-9 hours of sleep, if you don’t sleep enough your cortisol levels will be increased and hence lowers your testosterone levels which will adversely affect your muscles. Dedicate yourself to these workout routines, the more dedication you have the fastest you will gain muscles.
